Ultimate Burger

Description

Enjoy some homemade fast food with this Ultimate Burger

Ingredients

  • Beef Burgers
  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Bugers and bacon
  2. Cook onions
  3. melt cheese ontop of buger
  4. toast buns
  5. construct burger
  6. add sauce

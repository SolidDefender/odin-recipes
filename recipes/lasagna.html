Lasagne

The World's Best Lasagna

This lasagna is bound to be a delicious treat for all the family!

Ingredients

  • Miced meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tinned tomatos
  • Spices
  • Sugar
  • Pasta sheets
  • Cheese

    • Steps

    1. Cook the meat, onions and garlic and sauce.
    2. Alternate layers of the sauce and pasta sheets
    3. Add white sauce and cheese to the top
    4. Bake at 200c for 45min
